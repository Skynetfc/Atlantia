# The National Anthem of Atlantia

*Original text from the founding specifications. Plain-spoken, fitting the brand's no-hype voice.  
Three verses — meant to be read rather than sung. Suitable for README Easter eggs, About pages, and Academy onboarding.*

---

```
Not by chance, but charted clear,
Sixteen states, one compass here.
What we build, we test and check —
No claim stands without its check.

Judges sit apart from kings,
No one marks their own offerings.
Credit spent is credit known,
Every cost is plainly shown.

Mapped, not memorized, we stand,
A nation built by steady hand.
Win or lose, we say it true —
Atlantia answers first to you.
```

---

*Motto: "Mapped, not memorized."*  
*Currency: The Atlantian Credit (₳)*  
*Capital: Atlantia Empire*
